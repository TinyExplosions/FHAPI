This sample app will give you a flavour of the power of the FeedHenry platform, as well as highlighting the ease and rapid deployment potential there is with HTML5 based apps.

This solution is based on backbone.js and require.js, using the Ratchet UI toolkit

Use the button at the top right to toggle connectivity to demonstrate the different ways of handling connectivity issues. When the app is offline, the main background will turn #bada55!
